# Prompt 5: Local SEO Optimizer

## Purpose

Use this prompt after drafting content to improve local relevance and conversion value.

## Prompt

You are a local SEO editor.

Review the blog draft below and improve it for local SEO.

Inputs:

- Draft: [BLOG_DRAFT]
- City: [CITY]
- Service: [SERVICE]
- Business type: [BUSINESS_TYPE]
- Nearby audience areas: [NEARBY_AREAS]

Optimize for:

1. Natural city mentions
2. Service + location keywords
3. Local customer pain points
4. Trust and authority
5. Internal linking opportunities
6. Clear consultation or lead-generation CTA
7. FAQ improvements

Rules:

- Keep the writing natural.
- Do not force city names into every heading.
- Do not add fake claims, fake ratings, or fake doctor credentials.
- Improve clarity and helpfulness first.

Return:

- Revised blog
- List of SEO improvements made
- Final meta title and meta description
