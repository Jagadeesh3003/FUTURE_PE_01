# AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

Prompt Engineering Task 3 by Future Interns

## Project Summary

This project builds a reusable AI prompt workflow for creating SEO-focused blog content clusters for business websites. The chosen niche is **skin care**, with local SEO targeting for **Visakhapatnam**.

The deliverable includes:

- 1 pillar blog
- 5 supporting blog topics
- Keyword and search intent mapping
- Reusable prompt system
- Generated SEO-ready content
- Premium dark navy webpage for presentation

## Business Niche

**Client type:** Skin care clinic / dermatology-led skin treatment center  
**Location:** Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh  
**Primary business goal:** Generate local leads for consultations and skin treatments.

## Content Cluster Strategy

The pillar page targets a broad local keyword:  
**Best Skin Care Clinic in Visakhapatnam**

Supporting blogs target specific high-intent service and trust-building searches:

1. Acne treatment in Visakhapatnam
2. Chemical peel treatment in Visakhapatnam
3. Laser hair removal in Visakhapatnam
4. Skin pigmentation treatment in Visakhapatnam
5. Bridal skin care in Visakhapatnam

Each supporting article links back to the pillar blog and to related service pages, creating a clear internal linking structure.
