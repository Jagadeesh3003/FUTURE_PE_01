Tucked into the tree-lined streets of Indiranagar, Brew & Bloom was built for people who care what goes into their cup — and who want a space that feels like theirs.

Our beans are single-origin, our interiors are full of plants you'll want to photograph, and our menu shifts with the season so there's always a reason to come back.

Whether you're settling in for a three-hour work session or catching up with someone you've been meaning to meet — this is that place.

What's Brewing

Every cup, every bite —

made for the way you like to spend your time.

01 —

Single-Origin Pour Over

Slow-brewed to order using beans from farms in Coorg, Chikmagalur, and rotating international origins. Each cup carries the distinct character of where it was grown — earthy, floral, or bright.

✦ Perfect for coffee drinkers who want to taste the difference, not just feel the caffeine.

02 —

Signature Bloom Latte

Our house espresso pulled to a tight, caramel-edged shot, layered under velvety steamed milk and finished with a hand-poured bloom pattern. The one Indiranagar regulars order without looking at the menu.

✦ Perfect for first-time visitors and regulars who need something reliable and genuinely good.

03 —

Seasonal Brunch Plate

A rotating menu built around what's locally available and worth eating right now. Fresh sourdough, thoughtfully seasoned eggs, something green, and a side that surprises you. Changed every 6–8 weeks.

✦ Perfect for weekend visitors and remote workers taking a proper lunch break.

04 —

Work-Friendly Table Package

Reserve a corner table for up to four hours — includes two beverages, high-speed Wi-Fi, and access to our quiet side of the café. For focused work without the guilt of nursing one cold coffee.

✦ Perfect for freelancers, startup folks, and students whose home office needs a change.

Our Promise

Every bean we use is traceable, every ingredient is chosen with care, and every person who works here is trained to treat your order like it matters — because it does. We're not a chain trying to feel local. We're local, full stop. Brew & Bloom exists because Bangalore deserves a café that takes both its coffee and its community seriously.
