# Prompt 01 — Homepage Copy

## 🎯 Purpose
Generate homepage copy with a strong headline, sub-headline, and intro paragraph for a local café.

---

## 📋 Prompt Template

```
You are a professional website copywriter specializing in local businesses.

Write homepage copy for the following business:

Business Name: [BUSINESS NAME]
Business Type: Café / Coffee Shop
Location: [AREA], [CITY]
Target Audience: [AUDIENCE]
Unique Selling Point: [USP]
Tone: Warm, friendly, inviting — like a place you want to spend hours in

Generate the following sections:
1. Hero Headline (max 10 words, benefit-driven, no clichés)
2. Sub-headline (one sentence — who it's for and what they get)
3. Intro Section (3–4 sentences — what makes this café special, local feel, emotion)
4. "Why Us" Section (3 short bullet points with a header each)

Rules:
- No generic phrases like "Welcome to our café" or "We serve the best coffee"
- Make it feel human, local, and specific to Bangalore
- Write copy that is ready to paste directly onto a website
```

---

## ✅ Filled Prompt Used

```
You are a professional website copywriter specializing in local businesses.

Write homepage copy for the following business:

Business Name: Brew & Bloom Café
Business Type: Café / Coffee Shop
Location: Indiranagar, Bangalore
Target Audience: Young professionals, students, remote workers, and weekend explorers
Unique Selling Point: Specialty single-origin coffee, plant-filled interiors, and a menu that changes with the season

Tone: Warm, friendly, inviting — like a place you want to spend hours in

Generate the following sections:
1. Hero Headline (max 10 words, benefit-driven, no clichés)
2. Sub-headline (one sentence — who it's for and what they get)
3. Intro Section (3–4 sentences — what makes this café special, local feel, emotion)
4. "Why Us" Section (3 short bullet points with a header each)

Rules:
- No generic phrases like "Welcome to our café" or "We serve the best coffee"
- Make it feel human, local, and specific to Bangalore
- Write copy that is ready to paste directly onto a website
```

---

## 🔁 How to Reuse This Prompt
Replace the bracketed fields for any other local business:
- `[BUSINESS NAME]` → Your client's business name
- `[AREA], [CITY]` → Their location
- `[AUDIENCE]` → Their target customers
- `[USP]` → What makes them different
- Adjust tone as needed (professional / bold / playful)
