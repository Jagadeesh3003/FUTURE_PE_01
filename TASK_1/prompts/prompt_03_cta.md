# Prompt 03 — CTA (Call-to-Action) Sections

## 🎯 Purpose
Generate persuasive CTA sections that drive visitors to visit, book, or contact the business.

---

## 📋 Prompt Template

```
You are a conversion copywriter for local business websites.

Write 3 CTA sections for the following business:

Business Name: [BUSINESS NAME]
Business Type: [TYPE]
Location: [AREA], [CITY]
Primary Action: [What you want visitors to do — visit, book, call, order]
Tone: [TONE]

Generate:
1. Primary Hero CTA (appears on homepage — headline + 1 sentence + button text)
2. Trust-Building CTA (mid-page — includes social proof or local credibility)
3. Urgency/Location CTA (footer area — location-based or time-sensitive)

Rules:
- Button text must be action-oriented (not just "Click Here" or "Submit")
- Include at least one local reference (Bangalore / area name)
- Make the reader feel they're missing out if they don't act
- Keep copy short and punchy
```

---

## ✅ Filled Prompt Used

```
You are a conversion copywriter for local business websites.

Write 3 CTA sections for the following business:

Business Name: Brew & Bloom Café
Business Type: Specialty Coffee Café
Location: Indiranagar, Bangalore
Primary Action: Visit the café, follow on Instagram, reserve a table for the weekend
Tone: Friendly, warm, with a slight FOMO edge

Generate:
1. Primary Hero CTA (appears on homepage — headline + 1 sentence + button text)
2. Trust-Building CTA (mid-page — includes social proof or local credibility)
3. Urgency/Location CTA (footer area — location-based or time-sensitive)

Rules:
- Button text must be action-oriented (not just "Click Here" or "Submit")
- Include at least one local reference (Bangalore / Indiranagar)
- Make the reader feel they're missing out if they don't act
- Keep copy short and punchy
```

---

## 🔁 How to Reuse This Prompt
- Change `Primary Action` based on the business goal (book appointment, enquire, buy, call)
- Adjust urgency triggers: "limited seats", "this weekend only", "new batch starting"
- Add seasonal hooks: "Monsoon special", "Summer offer"
