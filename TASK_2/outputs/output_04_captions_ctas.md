## 📣 CTA BANK — 10 Variations

---

**1. Soft Visit CTA**
> "Check them out when you get a chance — their page is worth five minutes of your time."

**2. Curiosity CTA**
> "Go to their profile and just see if something catches your eye. No pressure."

**3. Community CTA**
> "Tag a friend who keeps saying they have nothing to wear."

**4. Urgency CTA**
> "Festival season's here and the good pieces go fast — don't wait and then regret it."

**5. Social Follow CTA**
> "Follow their Instagram — they drop new stuff regularly and it sells out quietly."

**6. Location CTA (Hyderabad-specific)**
> "If you're in Hyderabad, just go in person once — Banjara Hills / Madhapur area. Worth the trip."

**7. Discount / Offer CTA**
> "They run occasional offers — follow so you don't miss the next one."

**8. Story / Link CTA**
> "Link's in the bio — takes 30 seconds to browse and you'll probably find something."

**9. Question CTA (Comment-bait)**
> "What's your go-to style — streetwear, fusion, or occasion? Drop it below, curious."

**10. FOMO CTA**
> "Half my friend group already knows about this. Now you do too — don't be the last one."

---

## 📊 CTA Selection Guide

| Situation | Best CTA |
|-----------|----------|
| Cold audience, first time seeing brand | Curiosity CTA (#2) |
| Warm audience, already engaged | Soft Visit (#1) or Link CTA (#8) |
| Festival / seasonal campaign | Urgency CTA (#4) |
| Reach / awareness campaign | Community CTA (#3) or FOMO (#10) |
| Growing followers | Social Follow CTA (#5) |
| Driving engagement | Question CTA (#9) |
