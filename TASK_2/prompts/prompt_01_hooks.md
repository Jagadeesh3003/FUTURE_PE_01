# Prompt 01 — Hook Generation

## 🎯 Purpose
Generate 5 scroll-stopping hooks for a UGC ad. The hook is the first 3 seconds — it decides whether someone watches or scrolls.

---

## 📋 Prompt Template

```
You are a UGC content strategist and conversion copywriter.

Generate 5 different scroll-stopping hooks for the following brand:

Brand Name: [BRAND NAME]
Product/Service: [PRODUCT TYPE]
Target Audience: [AUDIENCE]
Platform: [Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts / Facebook Reels]
Tone: Real, raw, authentic — like a customer talking to a friend

Hook Types to Cover (one each):
1. Pain Point Hook — Opens with a frustration the audience feels
2. Curiosity Hook — Makes them wonder "wait, what?"
3. Social Proof Hook — Leads with a result or crowd reaction
4. Contrast Hook — Before vs. after or "I used to... until..."
5. Bold Claim Hook — A surprising or counter-intuitive statement

Rules:
- Each hook must be under 15 words
- Must feel like something a real person would say on camera
- No brand names in the hook (let it feel organic)
- No exclamation marks — keep it conversational
- Hooks should work as a spoken on-camera line
```

---

## ✅ Filled Prompt Used

```
You are a UGC content strategist and conversion copywriter.

Generate 5 different scroll-stopping hooks for the following brand:

Brand Name: Threads & Thread Co.
Product/Service: Affordable fusion clothing — Indo-western, streetwear, occasion wear
Target Audience: Women and men aged 18–30 in Hyderabad — college students, young professionals
Platform: Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels
Tone: Real, raw, authentic — like a Hyderabad local talking to their friend group

Hook Types to Cover (one each):
1. Pain Point Hook — Opens with a frustration the audience feels
2. Curiosity Hook — Makes them wonder "wait, what?"
3. Social Proof Hook — Leads with a result or crowd reaction
4. Contrast Hook — Before vs. after or "I used to... until..."
5. Bold Claim Hook — A surprising or counter-intuitive statement

Rules:
- Each hook must be under 15 words
- Must feel like something a real person would say on camera
- No brand names in the hook (let it feel organic)
- No exclamation marks — keep it conversational
- Hooks should work as a spoken on-camera line
- Add a Hyderabad/local flavour where natural
```

---

## 🔁 How to Reuse
- Change `[PRODUCT TYPE]` for any category (skincare, food, gadgets, services)
- Change `[AUDIENCE]` to shift age group, gender, or city
- Add "Focus on festival season hooks" or "Focus on college audience hooks" for niche versions
